Courses - Semester 3

Semester 1 Semester 2 Semester 3 Semester 4

Motion Graphics

Students will enhance already existing footage and will create animation sequences using Adobe After Effects. Students will work with many different forms of media, including video and audio. Adding transitions and effects to create professional looking videos.

Web Production

Students will learn how to overcome the many common problems that all web developers need to fix. Continuing on the skills learnt in the first two semesters, students will learn how to boost their productivity levels by using a diverse number of industry standard tools and frameworks.

Server-Side Web Development  

Students will build on the importance of responsive websites through their experience in the CMS class last semester by leveraging databases to store and output content and change the website’s content and interface based on user interactions. Students will focus on the creation of dynamic data and APIs used with responsive sites.

Applied Projects I

Students will be provided with an opportunity to work with real clients, using their skills to build projects in a real-world situation. Students will be working in teams, planning and developing a project, going through the full project cycle. From the project proposal to final presentation, students will be developing their teamwork skills, and will be using the best practises learnt in the Project Management Skills class.

Project Management Skills

Students will learn the crucial skills needed to succeed any project. This includes teamwork, and effective management of time, and resources as well as clients. Students will be taught the theory of project management, learning the industry standard guidelines and best practices. Students will explore the full project life cycle ; planning, risk management, IP rights, team and client communication, project scope, project documentation and closure.
