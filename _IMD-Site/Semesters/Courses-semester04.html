Courses - Semester 4

Semester 1 Semester 2 Semester 3 Semester 4

Communications II

Continuing what was learnt in the second semester, students will be able to write correctly and clearly communicate on web-based content and speak with confidence to a variety of audiences. Students will be able to promote themselves efficiently with the skills taught in this class. Students will use workplace scenarios, and identify their communication strengths and weaknesses.

Applied Projects II

Students will continue to work in teams to make client projects. These projects will be more challenging to test the students on what they have learned in past semesters. At the end of the semester students will make a presentation of their final work.

Digital Publishing

Students will explore the creation process of digital publication in many different formats, building on layouts, design and software knowledge. As well as current trends, and the directions of the industry. Students will embed graphics, video, audio, and animations into digital publications.

Media Production

Students will practise the entirety of the video production cycle. In and out of studio, with an emphasis on the preproduction and production phases.

Web Video Production

Students will focus on creating online video channels such as YouTube or Vimeo. Students will also examine a variety of tools used to create videos; drones, mounted cameras, and multi-camera operations.

Web Applications

Students will explore libraries, frameworks, new web technologies, and more advanced coding techniques. Students will leverage the capabilities of modern browsers, though server and client side scripting, to provide richer user experiences.

Content Management Systems II

Students will explore how a CMS can be used in the public and private sectors, and how they can leverage development and design skills for creating templates, plugins and many other add-ons.
