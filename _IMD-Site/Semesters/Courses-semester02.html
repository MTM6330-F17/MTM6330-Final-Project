Courses - Semester 2

Semester 1 Semester 2 Semester 3 Semester 4

Comunications I

Students will learn how to effectively communicate through a mix of exercises, lectures, and independent study. Students will practice reading, writing, speaking, and listening in the english language. As well as, documenting information, and using technology to communicate like a professional.

Video Foundations

In this class, students will learn the full production cycle for video projects. From the pre-production to production and then post-production, students will ensure the finest quality result when going to post-production to make as less corrections during the editing stage . Video editing and audio tools will be introduced.

Client Side Web Development

Building on the knowledge acquired during the last semester, students will learn Javascript and jQuery. As well as technologies related to the client side to make interactive websites. Students will also be taught best practices and will be introduced to core coding concepts such as conditional statements, functions, and loops.

Content Management Systems I

During this class, students will learn how to build websites using content management systems. Specifically Wordpress, Joomla!, and Drupal. CMS’ have pre-build functionality that offer developers and designers a quick way to build modern, feature-rich, and responsive websites. Students will be taught how to install, configure, customize and style these websites. Also, students will learn when and how to use a CMS and how it can benefit the client.

User Experience Design

Students will go beyond the basics of creating user interfaces and simple graphics. Students will be focusing on how interfaces should react to the users expectations. Students will be designing user interfaces that allow for improved workflow while linking what was learned in the last semester by gathering user feedback and data. Students will also be introduced to practical and useful planning techniques for creating user interfaces..
