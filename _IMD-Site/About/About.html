About IMD

This is a two year program is designed to prepare students for many different jobs in the digital media industry. Students will learn the base elements of planning media projects as well as the development stage and the creative and technical aspects. In this hands-on program, students will explore web development, digital imaging, authoring, photography as well as video. Students will work on individual and team projects and gain practical experience working with clients in the applied projects course.
